Our Story

Crafting quality aluminium and glazing solutions with integrity and skill.

Founded in 2010, Finlay Aluminium & Glazing Works has grown from a small workshop to a trusted construction partner across Ghana. Our focus is on precision, durability and design-led solutions for both public and private sectors.

We combine experienced craftsmen, modern fabrication techniques and project management skills to deliver projects on time and within budget. Our team values safety, sustainability and long-term client relationships.
